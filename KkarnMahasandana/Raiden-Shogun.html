Detail:
Raiden Shogun is the main DPS in the team. The DMG of Elemental Burst of Raiden Shogun is maximized by Kujou Sara(C6)’s Electro CRIT DMG Bonus and Kazuha’s DMG buff/Resistance Reduction. 
 
Raiden Shogun can boost Energy Recharge of their teammates by using Elemental Burst, and also increase DMG of teammates’ Elemental Burst by using Elemental Skill. This makes Raiden Shogun a all-rounded Main DPS.
